## Overview

Stripe integration enables online credit card payments on your invoices. Clients can pay instantly with major credit cards, payments are processed automatically, and invoice status updates in real-time.

**Why Use Stripe:**
- Clients pay 5-10x faster with instant payment option
- Automatic payment tracking
- Secure payment processing
- Professional payment experience
- Standard industry rates

## Stripe Requirements

### What You Need

**Business Information:**
- Legal business name
- Business address
- Tax ID/EIN (or SSN for sole proprietors)
- Business type (LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietor, etc.)

**Banking Information:**
- Business bank account
- Account and routing numbers
- Bank account ownership verification

**Identity Verification:**
- Government-issued ID
- Business ownership documentation
- May require additional documents for certain business types

<Note>
  You can start accepting payments during verification. Payouts may be held until full verification complete.
</Note>

## Setting Up Stripe

### Initial Connection

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Payments**
2. Click **Connect Stripe Account**
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe's website
4. Choose: **Create new Stripe account** or **Connect existing account**

**If Creating New Account:**
5. Enter email and create password
6. Provide business information
7. Add bank account details
8. Submit identity documents
9. Complete onboarding
10. Return to Every AI

**If Connecting Existing:**
5. Log in to your Stripe account
6. Authorize Every AI access
7. Return to Every AI

**Setup Complete:**
- Connection status shown in Settings
- Online payments now available on invoices
- Test by creating and viewing sample invoice

<Tip>
  Most users complete basic setup in 10 minutes. Full verification takes 1-3 business days.
</Tip>

### Stripe Account Status

**Enabled:**
- ✅ Fully operational
- ✅ Accept payments
- ✅ Receive payouts
- ✅ No restrictions

**Payouts Disabled:**
- ✅ Can accept payments from clients
- ⚠️ Payouts on hold
- Action: Complete bank account setup
- Action: Finish identity verification

**Restricted:**
- ⚠️ Account under review
- May need additional information
- Check Stripe dashboard for details
- Contact Stripe support

**Not Connected:**
- ❌ No Stripe integration
- Cannot accept online payments
- Need to connect account

<Note>
  Both "Enabled" and "Payouts Disabled" allow clients to pay. Focus on completing full onboarding for payouts.
</Note>

## Using Stripe for Payments

### Enabling Online Payments

**On New Invoice:**
- Online payments enabled by default
- Client sees "Pay Now" button
- Can disable per invoice if needed

**On Existing Invoice:**
- Edit draft invoice
- Toggle "Enable Online Payments"
- Save changes

**What Client Sees:**
- Professional invoice page
- "Pay Now" button prominently displayed
- Secure Stripe payment form
- Accepted card types shown

### Payment Process

**Client Side:**
1. Opens invoice link
2. Reviews invoice details
3. Clicks **Pay Now** button
4. Enters credit card details (secure Stripe form)
5. Clicks **Pay $X.XX**
6. Payment processes instantly
7. Sees confirmation page
8. Receives email receipt

**Your Side:**
1. Receive instant notification email
2. Invoice status automatically updates to **Paid**
3. Payment record created
4. Activity timeline updated
5. Funds added to Stripe balance

<Tip>
  The entire process takes less than 60 seconds for clients. No invoicing delays!
</Tip>

## Stripe Processing Fees

### Standard Rates

**US & Canada:**
- **Credit Card**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **American Express**: Same as above (no premium)

**Fee Calculation Example:**
```
Invoice Amount: $1,000
Stripe Fee: ($1,000 × 0.029) + $0.30 = $29.30
You Receive: $970.70
```

**Who Pays Fees:**
- You (the business) pay the fees
- Fees deducted from payment automatically
- Net amount deposited to your account

**Volume Discounts:**
- Available for high-volume businesses
- Contact Stripe for custom pricing
- Requires significant transaction volume

<Note>
  Stripe fees are standard across industry. They're worth it for faster payment!
</Note>

### Fee Transparency

**Viewing Fees:**
1. Go to Stripe Dashboard
2. View **Payments** section
3. See gross amount and fee for each transaction
4. Net amount shown

**Accounting for Fees:**
- Fees are business expense (tax-deductible)
- Track in accounting software
- Include in expense reports
- Reduce profit margin slightly

<Tip>
  Factor processing fees into your pricing. Most businesses add 3% to rates to cover fees.
</Tip>

## Payouts & Banking

### How Payouts Work

**Payout Process:**
1. Client pays via Stripe
2. Funds collect in your Stripe balance
3. Stripe automatically initiates payout
4. Funds transfer to your bank account
5. Typically arrives in 2-7 business days

**Payout Schedule:**
- **Default**: Daily automatic payouts
- **Custom**: Weekly or monthly (configurable in Stripe)
- **Manual**: On-demand payouts (requires setup)

**Payout Timing:**
- First payout: 7-14 days (verification period)
- Subsequent payouts: 2 business days (US), 3-7 days (Canada)
- Weekends/holidays may delay

### Managing Bank Accounts

**In Stripe Dashboard:**
1. Log in to Stripe
2. Go to **Settings** → **Bank accounts and scheduling**
3. View connected bank accounts
4. Add additional accounts
5. Set default payout account
6. Configure payout schedule

**Multiple Bank Accounts:**
- Add backup account
- Route payouts to different accounts
- Useful for accounting separation

<Warning>
  Always verify bank account details. Incorrect routing/account numbers delay payouts!
</Warning>

### Payout Reports

**Track Payouts:**
- View in Stripe Dashboard
- Download payout statements
- See breakdown: gross, fees, net
- Reconcile with bank statements

**Accounting Integration:**
- Export payout data
- Provide to accountant
- Track revenue vs payments received

## Refunds & Disputes

### Processing Refunds

**To Refund Payment:**
1. Log in to Stripe Dashboard
2. Find payment in **Payments** section
3. Click payment
4. Click **Refund Payment**
5. Enter amount (full or partial)
6. Add reason
7. Confirm refund

**What Happens:**
- Funds returned to client's card (5-10 business days)
- Stripe fees NOT refunded to you
- Every AI invoice NOT automatically updated
- Update invoice manually (void or note refund)

**Partial Refunds:**
- Refund any amount up to original
- Useful for adjustments or credits
- Multiple partial refunds allowed

<Warning>
  Refunds don't update Every AI invoices automatically. Void invoice or add note about refund.
</Warning>

### Disputes & Chargebacks

**If Client Disputes:**
1. Stripe notifies you via email
2. You receive chargeback notification
3. Funds held or withdrawn pending resolution
4. You have 7-14 days to respond

**How to Respond:**
1. Log in to Stripe Dashboard
2. Go to disputed payment
3. Upload evidence:
   - Invoice
   - Delivery confirmation
   - Client correspondence
   - Terms and conditions
   - Any relevant documentation
4. Submit response

**Outcomes:**
- **Win**: Funds returned to you
- **Lose**: Funds returned to client, chargeback fee applied
- **Partial**: Sometimes partial refund negotiated

<Tip>
  Good documentation prevents disputes. Keep records of all client communications and deliverables!
</Tip>

## Security & Compliance

### PCI Compliance

**Good News:** You're automatically PCI compliant!

**Why:**
- Stripe handles all card data
- No card details stored on Every AI servers
- Payment form hosted securely by Stripe
- Compliance burden on Stripe, not you

### Data Security

**Security Features:**
- HTTPS encryption
- Stripe-hosted payment forms
- No card data exposure
- Secure token-based integration
- Two-factor authentication (Stripe login)

### Fraud Protection

**Stripe Radar:**
- Automatic fraud detection
- Machine learning algorithms
- Blocks suspicious payments
- Reduces chargebacks

**Best Practices:**
- Verify large or unusual payments
- Contact client if payment flagged
- Document all work/deliverables
- Clear service descriptions on invoices

## Troubleshooting

### Client Can't Complete Payment

**Common Issues:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card declined by bank
- Incorrect card details
- Address verification failure
- Payment flagged as fraud

**Solutions:**
- Ask client to contact their bank
- Try different card
- Verify billing address matches card
- Contact Stripe support for fraud flags

### Payout Not Received

**Check:**
- Payout initiated? (view Stripe Dashboard)
- Correct bank account?
- Processing time (2-7 business days)
- Bank account status (closed/frozen?)

**Solutions:**
- Wait full processing time
- Verify bank details in Stripe
- Contact Stripe support
- Contact your bank

### Connection Lost

**Reconnecting:**
1. Settings → Payments
2. Click **Reconnect Stripe**
3. Log in to Stripe
4. Reauthorize Every AI
5. Connection restored
