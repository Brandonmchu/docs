## March 2026

### Voice & Tone

Released March 25, 2026.

Every Agent now has a dedicated **Voice & Tone** workspace that helps it write more consistently on behalf of your business.

**What's new:**
- **Audience-specific voice profiles** — separate guidance for client-facing, internal/casual, and non-client external writing
- **Generated from email history** — Every can build an initial voice profile from how your business already communicates
- **Editable tone controls** — refine the description, tags, and example snippets for each audience
- **Used only when relevant** — the profile is applied to drafting flows without bloating everyday operational chat

**How to use it:**
[Agent Settings → Voice & Tone](https://every.ai/agent/settings/voice-tone) → Review generated profiles → Edit or refresh as needed

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** "Draft this proposal in my normal client-facing tone" or "Write a follow-up note that sounds like us"
</Tip>

### Branded Client Portal & Public URLs

Released March 10, 2026.

Every now supports a branded client-facing surface across your portal, invoices, proposals, and booking flow.

**What's new:**
- **Branded subdomain** — use `{your-slug}.every.ai` for client-facing links
- **Client portal** — contacts can log in with a magic link to view their invoices and proposals
- **Shared public surface** — invoices, proposals, and booking can all live under the same branded domain
- **Portal shortcuts** — direct links for invoice and proposal sections on the portal
- **Backward compatibility** — root-domain public links still work, while branded links become the preferred experience

**How to use it:**
Set your subdomain slug in organization settings, then share branded links like `{your-slug}.every.ai`, `{your-slug}.every.ai/invoice/{public_id}`, `{your-slug}.every.ai/proposal/{public_id}`, and `{your-slug}.every.ai/book`

<Tip>
  Use the client portal when you want clients to revisit invoices and proposals without digging through old email threads.
</Tip>

### Client Booking Page

Released March 5, 2026.

Let clients book appointments directly through your public booking page at `{your-slug}.every.ai/book`.

**What's new:**
- **Public booking page** — clients select a service, pick a date and time, fill in details, and pay if required
- **3 booking modes** — Standard (date + time + payment), Free (no payment), and Schedule Later (you coordinate timing after)
- **Flexible payment collection** — no payment, pay at booking via Stripe Checkout, or save card and charge before service
- **Weekly availability + overrides** — set recurring hours and block off specific dates
- **Google Calendar integration** — block busy slots automatically and create calendar events on booking
- **Automated emails** — confirmation, 24-hour reminder, 1-hour reminder, cancellation, and reschedule notifications
- **Client self-service** — clients cancel or reschedule via a secure link (no login needed)
- **Deep data integration** — uses your existing Services, auto-creates Contacts and Clients, auto-creates paid Invoices
- **Booking policy editor** — add cancellation terms and instructions with rich text formatting

**How to use it:**
[Booking Settings](https://every.ai/clients/bookings/settings) → Select bookable services → Set availability → Share your link

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** "Create a booking for Sarah Chen next Tuesday at 2pm" or "Show me all upcoming bookings this week"
</Tip>

## December 2024

### Client Contacts System

Manage multiple contacts per client with smart email routing.

**What's new:**
- Add unlimited contacts per client (name, email, phone, job title)
- Set different recipients for invoices vs proposals - send invoices to billing while proposals go to the CEO
- Assign roles: Primary (TO), CC, or excluded from emails
- Each contact can have their own social links (LinkedIn, Twitter, website)

**How to use it:**
Open any client → Edit → Add Contact → Set their invoice and proposal roles

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** "Add a contact John Smith to ACME Corp as the primary invoice recipient"
</Tip>

### Enhanced Client Enrichment

AI-powered enrichment now discovers more data from multiple sources.

**What's new:**
- Discovers additional contacts at a company by scanning your Gmail (last 180 days) and Google Contacts
- Pulls social profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter) automatically
- Choose enrichment mode: "Fill Empty" (preserve your data) or "Overwrite" (update everything)
- Auto-converts individual clients to business type when multiple contacts are found

**How to use it:**
Open a client → Click **Enrich Client** → Choose your mode → Review and approve

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** "Enrich ACME Corp and find additional contacts from my emails"
</Tip>

### Google Contacts Sync

See at a glance which contacts already exist in your team's Google Contacts.

**What's new:**
- Sync indicator badge appears on contacts that match entries in your Google Contacts
- Know when colleagues have already worked with a contact
- Daily background sync keeps status current
- Email autocomplete pulls suggestions from your Google Contacts as you type

**How to use it:**
Connect Google Workspace → Create or view contacts → Look for the sync indicator

### Payment Notifications & Reminders

Keep clients informed with professional payment communications.

**What's new:**
- **Batch confirmations:** Select multiple manual payments → Send confirmation emails to all at once (up to 100)
- **Payment reminders:** Send reminder emails for overdue invoices with one click
- Emails include invoice details, amount, payment link (if online payments enabled)

**How to use it:**
- Confirmations: Payments page → Select payments → Send Confirmation Emails
- Reminders: Open invoice → Send Payment Reminder

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** "Send payment reminders to all invoices overdue by more than 7 days"
</Tip>

### Social & Web Links

Add LinkedIn, websites, and other links to clients and contacts.

**What's new:**
- Attach links to clients or individual contacts
- Supported: Website, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, GitHub
- Often populated automatically during enrichment

**How to use it:**
Open client or contact → Links section → Add Link
