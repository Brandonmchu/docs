## Overview

Every provides reporting and analytics to track business performance, key metrics, and make data-driven decisions.

## Dashboard

**Key Metrics:** Total revenue, outstanding amount, overdue amount, draft invoices, monthly totals

**Quick Stats:** Paid/unpaid ratio, average invoice amount, average days to payment

**Activity Timeline:** Recent invoices, payments, proposals, automations, agent actions

## Available Reports

### Revenue Tracking

**Includes:** Total revenue, revenue by client/offering, trends, month-over-month, year-over-year

**Date Ranges:** Today, week, month, quarter, year, custom

**Visualizations:** Line charts (time), bar charts (client), pie charts (service)

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Track revenue metrics:
  - "Show me my total revenue for Q4 2024"
  - "What was my revenue last month?"
  - "Create a revenue report by client for this year"
</Tip>

### Outstanding Invoices

**Shows:** All unpaid invoices, total outstanding, overdue vs current, aged receivables (30/60/90+ days), client breakdown

**Actions:** Send bulk reminders, identify problem clients, forecast cash flow, prioritize collections

<Tip>
  Focus collection on 31-60 day bucket - not too early, not too late!
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Monitor unpaid invoices:
  - "Which clients owe me money?"
  - "Show me all overdue invoices"
  - "What's my total outstanding balance?"
</Tip>

### Client Payment History

**Per-Client Metrics:** Total paid, outstanding, avg days to payment, on-time rate, invoice count

**Use for:** Identify reliable clients, flag slow payers, adjust payment terms

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Analyze client payment patterns:
  - "How quickly does ACME Corp usually pay invoices?"
  - "Show me which clients are the slowest payers"
</Tip>

### Expense Summaries

**Reports:** Total by period, by category, billable vs non-billable, billed vs unbilled, tax-deductible totals

**Client Expenses:** Per-client spending, billback status, unbilled value

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Analyze your expenses:
  - "What were my total expenses last quarter?"
  - "Show me unbilled expenses for ACME Corp"
</Tip>

### Time Tracking

**Reports:** Total hours, billable vs non-billable, by client/service, utilization rate, billed vs unbilled, revenue from time

**Utilization:** Billable Hours รท Total Hours (e.g., 30/40 = 75%)

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Track billable hours:
  - "How many billable hours did I work this month?"
  - "Show me unbilled time entries for ACME Corp"
  - "What's my utilization rate for Q4?"
</Tip>

### Tax Reports

**Sales Tax:** Total collected, by rate/client, remittance due, filing summaries

**Expense Tax:** Total deductible, by category, receipt status, year-end summary

### Payment Reports

**Metrics:** Total received, payment methods, average amount, timing (days to payment), online vs manual

**Trends:** Payment velocity, preferred methods, seasonal patterns

## Filters & Customization

**Date Ranges:** Today, yesterday, week, month, quarter, year, custom

**Client Filters:** Specific client, client group, active vs archived, high-value

**Status Filters:**
- Invoice: Draft, issued, paid, overdue, void
- Payment: Unpaid, partially paid, fully paid, overdue

**Export:** CSV (Excel), PDF (printable), JSON (integrations)

<Tip>
  Export to CSV for custom Excel/Sheets analysis!
</Tip>

## Performance Metrics

**Financial KPIs:**
- **MRR**: Monthly recurring revenue from automations/retainers
- **Average Invoice Value**: Total revenue รท invoice count
- **Collection Rate**: Payments collected รท invoices issued
- **DSO**: Days sales outstanding (avg days to payment)

**Operational KPIs:** Invoice volume, client acquisition, client retention, utilization rate

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Calculate key metrics:
  - "What's my average invoice amount?"
  - "What's my monthly recurring revenue?"
</Tip>

**Trends:** Revenue growth, client base growth, deal size changes, payment speed, expense growth

**Visualizations:** Trend lines, moving averages, period comparisons, forecasts

## Client Analytics

**Top Clients:** Ranked by total revenue, period revenue, invoice count, avg invoice, payment speed

<Tip>
  **Ask Every Agent:** Identify top clients:
  - "Who are my top 5 clients by revenue?"
  - "Show me my most valuable clients"
</Tip>

**Risk Analysis:** Identify slow payers, increasing days to payment, disputed invoices, reduced volume, growing balances

**Actions:** Proactive communication, adjust terms, request deposits, re-evaluate relationships

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Review weekly** - Set recurring calendar event for key metrics.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Track trends** - Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons reveal more than snapshots.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Focus on action** - Use reports to drive decisions, not just observe.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Monitor cash flow** - Outstanding invoices report is crucial for cash management.
</Tip>

## Common Reports

**Weekly:** Outstanding invoices, new payments, overdue invoices, draft invoices

**Monthly:** Total revenue, month-over-month, top clients, expenses, tax collected

**Quarterly:** Revenue trends, client retention, avg invoice value, collection performance, expense analysis

**Year-End:** Annual revenue, tax reports, client performance, service performance, full expense report
