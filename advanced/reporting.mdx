### Outstanding Invoices Report

Track unpaid invoices:

**Shows:**
- All issued, unpaid invoices
- Total outstanding amount
- Overdue vs current
- Aged receivables (30/60/90+ days)
- Client breakdown

**Actions:**
- Send reminders in bulk
- Identify problem clients
- Cash flow forecasting
- Collection priorities

**Aging Buckets:**
- Current (not yet due)
- 1-30 days overdue
- 31-60 days overdue
- 61-90 days overdue
- 90+ days overdue

<Tip>
  Focus collection efforts on 31-60 day bucket - not too early, not too late!
</Tip>

## Performance Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Financial KPIs:**
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: From automations and retainers
- **Average Invoice Value**: Total revenue / number of invoices
- **Collection Rate**: Payments collected / invoices issued
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**: Average days to collect payment

**Operational KPIs:**
- **Invoice Volume**: Number of invoices per period
- **Client Acquisition**: New clients added
- **Client Retention**: Repeat clients percentage
- **Utilization Rate**: Billable hours / total hours

### Performance Trends

**Trend Analysis:**
- Revenue growth rate
- Client base growth
- Average deal size change
- Payment speed trends
- Expense growth

**Visualizations:**
- Trend lines
- Moving averages
- Period comparisons
- Forecasts (based on trends)

## Client Analytics

### Top Clients Report

Identify your most valuable clients:

**Ranked By:**
- Total revenue (all-time)

- Number of invoices
- Average invoice amount

**Insights:**

Identify potential issues:

**Risk Indicators:**

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Review reports weekly** - Set a recurring calendar event to check key metrics.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Track trends, not just snapshots** - Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons reveal more than single period data.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Focus on actionable metrics** - Use reports to drive decisions, not just observe.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Export for deeper analysis** - Use Excel/Sheets for custom calculations and what-if scenarios.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Share with stakeholders** - Export reports for partners, investors, or accountants.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Set goals based on data** - Use historical performance to set realistic targets.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Monitor cash flow** - Outstanding invoices report is crucial for cash flow management.
</Tip>
