## What is Every?

Every is a business platform for firms that sell services, products, or a mix of both. Whether you bill hourly, sell fixed packages, run recurring client work, or let clients book time directly, Every helps you:

- **Manage clients and contacts** with AI-powered enrichment, LinkedIn import, and Google Contacts integration
- **Organize services and products** with reusable pricing, tax defaults, and structured catalogs
- **Create professional invoices** with branded templates and online payments
- **Track time and expenses** with built-in tools and receipt scanning
- **Automate recurring billing** with flexible scheduling and testing
- **Get paid faster** with integrated Stripe payment processing
- **Leverage AI** to streamline your workflow and make smart business decisions
